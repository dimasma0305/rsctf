# Player guide

This guide explains the normal competition flow from registration to the final scoreboard. Your event organizer may disable or rename some options.

## The usual flow

1. **Create an account** and sign in.
2. **Create or join a team** from the Teams page.
3. Open **Games**, choose the event, and join with one of your teams.
4. Wait for organizer approval if the game or division requires review.
5. Read the game notices and rules before opening challenges.
6. Solve challenges and submit flags from the challenge panel.
7. Follow the scoreboard, event feed, and any new notices during the event.

## Pick your game mode

| Mode | Your goal | Start here |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Jeopardy | Solve independent challenges and submit their flags | [Jeopardy games](./jeopardy) |
| Attack & Defense | Protect your services while stealing current flags from other teams | [Attack & Defense](./attack-defense) |
| King of the Hill | Take control of a shared service and keep your token in the marker | [King of the Hill](./koth) |

A game can contain more than one kind of challenge. Always follow the instructions on the individual challenge.

## Need help during an event?

Check, in order:

1. The event's **Notices** panel
2. The challenge description and hints
3. Your organizer's support channel
4. The [player troubleshooting notes](../reference/troubleshooting#player-problems)

Do not post live flags, private team tokens, VPN configurations, or challenge solutions in a public support channel.
